Traditional Textile Arts

Step into our virtual gallery dedicated to the rich tradition of Kazakh textile arts. Here you'll discover the incredible craftsmanship behind traditional carpet weaving, felt making, and embroidery that have been passed down through generations.

Our collection showcases the diverse techniques and patterns that make Kazakh textiles unique, from the geometric designs of syrmak carpets to the intricate patterns of tuskiiz wall hangings.

Jewelry & Metalwork Gallery

Welcome to our virtual exhibition of traditional Kazakh jewelry and metalwork. This space celebrates the sophisticated art of 'zerger oneri', showcasing the incredible skill and creativity of Kazakh jewelers throughout history.

Explore our collection of traditional jewelry pieces, from elaborate headdress decorations to intricate breast ornaments. Each piece demonstrates the mastery of techniques such as filigree, stone setting, and metal embossing.
